Reading List 2019

December 31, 2018 3 minutes read
Self • Books
reading • motivation

Heading into 2019, I aim to read a lot of books across genres - from historical fictions to the latest genetics developments, from philosophy about human societies to the realities of individual emotions.

Over the weekend, I updated my Goodreads shelves and downloaded a lot of “Samples” on my Kindle from the Amazon Store. My intention is to read these preview chapters at random on my Kindle and dive into only those books which interest me. serendipity ✨

With that said, I have also curated a list of must-read books for 2019:
