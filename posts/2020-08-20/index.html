Thought of the Day

The secret is in the line. - Charles Bukowski

This quote makes me think about a couple of ideas:

  • importance of scoping larger tasks/problems into simpler, action items.
  • building grander things is all about the process of working on smaller elements.

In a way, this somehow reminds me of the omnipresent notion of the Keep It Simple Stupid UNIX philosophy.
