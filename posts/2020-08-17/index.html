Keyboard Basics

After a break of 2 weeks, I picked up my keyboard practice back again. Just to get some practice, I have decided the first week solely on revisiting the tunes/melodies I have already learned. This has the added benefit of easing me into reading the music sheet again as well as providing me with small boosts of confidence in the initial phase.

TIL: I focussed mostly on the middle C position and practiced measures from these songs:

  • Beethoven’s Ode to Joy
  • Jingle Bells
  • Winds of Change
