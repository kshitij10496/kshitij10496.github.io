Elicit: Build a search engine, not a vector DB

December 23, 2023 One-minute read
notes

Ref: https://blog.elicit.com/search-vs-vector-db/

Really interesting take on the problem of knowledge discovery.

Full-text search still remains a challenging problem. At the same time, the increasing popularity of vector DBs, in particular, the embeddings data structure, provide another approach to the fundamental problem. A conjugation of both of these technologies can be used to build a valuable solution.

However, questions around the efficiency of such a solution remains to be tested and monitored.
