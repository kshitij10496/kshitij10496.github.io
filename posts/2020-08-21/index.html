Thought of the Day

“If you want to build a ship, don’t drum up the people to gather wood, divide the work, and give orders. Instead, teach them to yearn for the vast endless sea.” Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

Understanding the core of what truly motivates me is the first step to working towards achieving it. The story that the world can buy (hopefully) would be my raw ambition not just my talent. This quote reminds me of the scene from TVF Pitchers S01 when the protagonist (Naveen) presents his passion, grit and determination to an audience of investors. These meta qualities are often glorified in world culture, perhaps justifiably so - character builds curiosity. But that’s a thought for another day. For today, it’s enough to think a bit about the “vast endless sea” that I “yearn” for.
