About

Hey there! 👋

This is Kshitij, and you have reached my space on the World Wide Web. Currently, I’m based out of Berlin where I work as a software engineer mostly focusing on backend systems.

This blog is the place where I think in public. Words help shape my thoughts. Though they may be far from perfect, but this process of discovery and expression is valuable. The posts are my attempt to share my ideas, and reflect on experiences.

Note: I have deliberately disabled comments on my articles on this site.
It is neither my intention to refrain from feedback nor censor criticism of my writings.
However, I definitely desire to Make It Difficult to do so.

If you would like to have a conversation with me, you can schedule a call via Cal.com.
